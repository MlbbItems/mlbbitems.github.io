Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 December 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected in the MLBB Items app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in using Google, the app collects and stores the following information using Firebase Authentication and Firestore:

  • Unique user identifier (UID)
  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Account creation time

1.2 In-App Data

The app stores in-app data associated with your account, including virtual coins used to unlock features such as watermark removal.

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and Google AdMob to display ads. These services may collect:

  • Device information
  • App usage statistics
  • Advertising identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

Collected data is used to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Store in-app progress and virtual currency
  • Enable app features and functionality
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Display advertisements
  • Prevent abuse and fraud

3. Ads

The app displays ads using Google AdMob. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your Google account settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data is shared only with Google services (Firebase and AdMob) as required for app functionality.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All data is stored securely using Firebase infrastructure and security rules.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account remains active. If you stop using the app, your data may remain stored unless the account is deleted.
